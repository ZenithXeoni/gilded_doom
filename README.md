# Overview
This mod overhauls bartering, adds powerful new hammers, unique tomes with special effects, and the Gilded Brute armor set, introducing strategic combat mechanics, custom enchantments, and high-risk, high-reward gameplay for a more dynamic Minecraft experience. Inspired by Charter, this mod also has a Banishment System, allowing you to ban your foes (with a cost.). For the best experience, I heavily recommend combining this mod with [Enchancement](https://modrinth.com/mod/enchancement) as this mod is balanced around Enchancement PVP

## Barter Overhaul
As mentioned in the overview, I have indeed overhauled Bartering, here are some of the additions I made.

- 1-3 Netherite scraps
- Ancient Hammer
- Lodestone
- Mushroom Stew 
- Leather

For the full list I recommend turning the mod into a zip and looking at the `data` folder.

### Iron Hammer

- Has the Same Efficiency as a Netherite Pickaxe
- Damage is low compared to the other hammers
- Serves as an early-mid game weapon/pickaxe before you get your hands on an ancient hammer

### Ancient Hammer

- Obtained via piglin bartering or villager trading(Expert Weaponsmith).
- Attacking pulls mobs to the ground, does not apply fall damage to the pull unless they were really high up 
- Mining magma blocks has a 1% chance  to drop an Infernal Scrap
- With Torrent, right clicking sends you to the ground, if you are already on the ground, right clicking activates a shockwave and launches everyone within range up into the air, and if you are not sneaking, everyone within range(including you) gets dealt a heart of true damage. 

### Hellforged Hammer

- Has a 25% chance to Ignite the Target on fire for 20 seconds.
- Fiery Aura when holding

#### Enchant Changes:
- Fortitude grants strength and mining fatigue instead of resistance and slowness.
- Knockout ignites the Target on fire for 15 seconds. On proc

### Soulforged Hammer

- Obtained by Smithing the Hellforged Hammer with soul sand
- Ignites target on fire for 8 seconds
- Has a 25% chance to inflict Weakness for 5 Seconds
- Soul Fiery Aura when holding

#### Enchant Changes:
- Torrent inflicts Levitation 2, with the duration being dependent on the opponent's health, scaling down when their health is low.
- Fortitude grants weakness instead of slowness.
- Knockout inflicts 50% more blindness.

### Gilded Banisher (Without Ultimate Power)

- Can be used with a Brutal Tome
- Basically a netherite pickaxe with gold efficiency
- 25% chance to inflict Slowness 2 for a short amount of time
- Has a 5% chance to inflict Gilded Stasis for 2 seconds (Sorry not sorry)

## Gilded Brute Armor
 
- Helmet: While Sneaking you are granted Invis (full health required)
- Chestplate: Built-in Thorns III~. (With slightly reduced damage)
- Leggings: No extra stuff.
- Boots: no extra stuff.
- Full set: Haste 1 and Resistance 1 turn into lvl 2 with a 25% duration loss.
  - Haste Amplification applies when at 25% health or less
  - Resistance Amplification applies when at higher than 75% health

## Gilded Tomes
These tomes allow you to do certain things under set conditions.
| Tome Name           | Description           |
|---------------------|-----------------------|
| Brutal Tome            | When attacking while holding either a Golden Axe or Gilded Banisher in your main hand and the Brutal Tome in your off-hand, gain resistance 1 or haste 1 depending on your health. Strength is applied at lower than 50% HP, resistance is applied at 50% HP or higher. |
| Withering Tome        | while holding this Tome in your off-hand and using a stone sword or Soulforged Hammer, your attacks also grant wither on you but double the time on your target. |
| Immunity Tome  | While the Tome is in your offhand it will have a chance to protect you from Death's Mark. Right-clicking grants the Gilded Immunity effect with a 30 second duration, 3 minute cooldown|
| Tracking Tome | used to track players in the same world as you if you have an item with a soul in your main hand(requires Gilded Immunity, Gilded Empowerment, or Ultimate Power). Killing someone with the Tome in your offhand binds the opponent with the tome allowing you to track them without needing special effects by tracking with the tome in the main hand.|
| Cleansing Tome | Used to cure Pending Death, has different Enchants for different levels. Beware; Thou Shall Still Haunt (you will be given a curse that gives a debuff to you every time you get hurt [25% chance to proc], scaling depending on what type of pending death was removed) | 

## Effects
| Effect              | Description           |
|---------------------|-----------------------|

## Gilded Banisher:
### How to use Pending Death.
  
- You must first have a Gilded Banisher. Once you have that item, hitting someone has a 25% chance to inflict Death’s Mark. When someone is under Death’s Mark, and they get hit with your gilded banisher at Half Health, they receive Pending Death. 

- Once Pending Death is applied. Pending Death will either go off after time, or go off as soon as they get killed, so watch out for the bomb that comes after you kill them. 

## Purgatory Amalgam:
### How to activate Pending Purgatory.

Inflict pending death on your target or have another person do it for you and then Right Click with the Purgatory Amalgam.

This Amalgam is slightly broken and has currently has an unknown use besides making ducks suddenly appear.
