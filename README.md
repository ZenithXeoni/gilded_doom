# Overview
This mod overhauls bartering, adds powerful new hammers, unique tomes with special effects, and the Gilded Brute armor set, introducing strategic combat mechanics, custom enchantments, and high-risk, high-reward gameplay for a more dynamic Minecraft experience. Inspired by Charter, this mod also has a Banishment System, allowing you to ban your foes (with a cost.). For the best experience, I heavily recommend combining this mod with [Enchancement](https://modrinth.com/mod/enchancement) as this mod is balanced around Enchancement PVP

# Q&A

Q: When will you update/backport the mod to 1.x.x

_A: I do not have a specific update/backport plan, all updates/backports will be dependent on whether or not I wish to do so. you are free to update/backport my mod for as long as it is not publicly available._

Q: Can I include this in my modpack

_A: Yes, just make sure to credit me on the modrinth page_

Q: How can I contact you if I need something removed from the mod?

_A: If you are the rightful owner of content used in this mod and believe it should be removed, please contact me on discord (Handle is in the discord server) or at neptunite.horizons@gmail.com with proof of ownership and a clear reason for the request. (Example: You are the owner of [Charter](https://modrinth.com/mod/charter) and the Gilded Contract is way too similar to the Contract that you made. Write me an email or contact me via discord.)_


Q: I don’t like how the mod plays. Can I ask for a rework?

_A: The mod is designed with a specific vision in mind. If it doesn't fit your playstyle, you are welcome to modify it for personal use or suggest changes in my discord, but I am not obligated to implement every request. Additionally, if you are wanting a specific item removed from my mod, you can do so via [Item Obliterator](https://modrinth.com/mod/item-obliterator), and for enchants, I recommend either [Enchancement](https://modrinth.com/mod/enchancement) config or [Enchantment Disabler](https://modrinth.com/mod/enchantment-disabler)_
